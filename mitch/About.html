Project VI - About

Welcome to the about page for The Tower of Terror
This is where we will provide an overview of our project. In this project we will design an elevator system. The purpose of this project is to build and control and elevator, leveraging the use of a CAN bus coummincations environment, coupled with networked systems to create a means of tracking the elevators operational/diagnostics data. The car position of the elevator must also be viewable to computers over the Internet.
